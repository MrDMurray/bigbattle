Fair Question

A Google Classroom Seating Plan/Quiz App that is NOT BORING!

What does it do?

  • Loads your students from Google Classroom into a seating plan
  • A "Random Student" button for quiz questions
  • Dramatic music, sound effects
  • Reads out students names in a dramatic British AI voice
  • Hand picked organic reaction gifs to entertain!
  • Keeps track of points!
  • Custom Backgrounds!

You can enable or disable these features in settings (as well as make audio clips for student names).
